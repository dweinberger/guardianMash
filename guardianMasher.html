Guardian of the Shared.Eco

This is an amateur attempt at mashing up The Guardian and a new, under-development API for environmental information. Enter a search term and it will find the most recent articles in The Guardian about it. It will then look for related items in the new environmental API.

The related items are the ones after the green buttons with tags that eventually show up. It may take up to 15 seconds for any results to show up, especially in Chrome. (Firefox loads results incrementally.) It's all very preliminary. — May 22, 2017
